1. What We Collect
When you browse or shop with us, we may collect:
Personal info (that you provide):
  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Shipping/billing address
  • Phone number (if you give it)
  • Payment details (handled securely by our payment partners)
Technical info (collected automatically):
  • Your IP address
  • Browser type & device info
  • Pages you visit and time spent on them
  • Referral links (how you found us)

2. How We Use Your InfoWe only use your info to:
  • Process and ship your orders
  • Respond to messages or questions
  • Send updates or promotional emails (only if you subscribe)
  • Improve our site and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and keep things secure

3. Sharing Your Info
We keep your personal data private. We only share it with:
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
  • Shipping carriers (e.g., USPS, FedEx)
  • Email platforms (e.g., Mailchimp, if you subscribe)
  • Analytics tools (like Google Analytics)
  • Law enforcement or legal authorities, only if we must
We do not sell or trade your info, ever.

4. Cookies & Analytics
We use cookies (tiny bits of data stored on your device) to:
  • Keep your shopping cart working
  • Understand how visitors use our site
  • Improve speed, performance, and features
You can turn off cookies in your browser settings if you'd rather not allow them — just know some features might not work properly.

5. Your Rights
You’re in control of your data. At any time, you can:
  • Ask to see what info we have
  • Request changes or corrections
  • Delete your account or personal data (unless we need it for legal reasons)
  • Unsubscribe from our emails — just click the “unsubscribe” link


6. Keeping Your Info Safe
We use trusted platforms and tools to protect your information. While no online system is 100% foolproof, we work hard to keep your data secure and encrypted wherever possible.

7. For Customers Under 13
Our site isn’t meant for children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect info from anyone that young. If you’re a parent or guardian and think your child has shared info with us, contact us — we’ll take care of it right away.

8. Policy Changes
​We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in how we work or stay compliant with new laws. Any changes will be posted here with a new “last updated” date.
